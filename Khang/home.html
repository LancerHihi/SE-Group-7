About Us

  • At the Ho Chi Minh City University of Technology (HCMUT), we understand the importance of efficient and reliable printing services for both students and faculty.
  • Our campus is equipped with a variety of high-quality printers strategically placed across the university, ensuring easy access to printing facilities.
  • These printers cater to a wide range of needs, from academic materials to personal documents.
  • Whether you are printing assignments, research papers, or presentation slides, HCMUT provides a convenient and accessible solution.
  • We are committed to supporting the academic success of our community by offering reliable printing services that meet the diverse requirements of our students and staff.
